## Self-Publisher: User Stories

As an Author, I want...
    - To create downloadable ebooks from my digital manuscripts.
    - To update and delete manuscripts and their metadata.
    - To be able to track revisions and releases.
    - Some kind of DRM to protect against unauthorized copying.
    - To be able to obtain ISBNs, etc. easily.
    - To be able to hand out QR codes to contacts for promotional purposes.
    - To be able to track my downloads and sharing with an eye toward marketing.
    - My web storefront to integrate easily with my social media.

As a Customer, I want...
    - To browse ebooks in catalogs and by author. I want to search for authors and ebooks, and browse by genre, event, etc.
    - To be able to read ebooks on reader devices (Kindle, etc.) as well as in mobile apps and on the web.
    - Minimal friction around free downloads.
    - Account creation to be quick and easy.
    - Purchases to be secure and easy.
    - Downloads to be safe and fast.

As a Publisher, I want...
    - To be able to offer catalogs including works by multiple authors.
