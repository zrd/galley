## Self-Publisher

A self-publishing platform primarily for new and upcoming authors to easily share their work when networking and doing public appearances.

### Use cases
- Independent authors can publish a catalog of ebooks in a basic webstore format.
- Authors can do rudimentary formatting/editing but for the most part will have prepared their ebook for publishing on another platform.
- Authors can easily create samples and promotional products from their manuscripts.
- Authors can easily obtain ISBNs and other standard identifiers as needed.
- Authors can use basic features for free: Limited publishing, free downloads, possibly limited paid downloads.
- Authors can pay for premium features: Unlimited publishing, unlimited paid downloads, etc.
- Authors can easily share their products using QR codes, short links, etc. Links can include tracking per user (real world contact or anonymous), per event, etc.
- Authors can share their work in a natural, conversational flow when meeting fans and professional contacts at events like conventions, readings, festivals, etc.
- Authors can also use their webstore as a barebones outlet for their work, including pricing, very basic formatting/artwork, and other features to support publishing. Offering authors' works in print formats is not a priority, and any functionality to specifically support print should be postponed.
- Publishers or editors can publish a catalog of ebooks by multiple authors, collections of ebooks, etc.

### Requirements
- Support input/output formats: EPUB, PDF, .docx, ODF, other common ones?
- Allow paid and free downloads. Subscriptions would be nice to have.
- Provide ISBNs. For MVP, include ISBN as an object attribute, then add feature to obtain one automatically later.
